This work by Molexted is an adaptation of the Cube 2 map "disco" by Bonifarz. It is likewise licensed under the CC BY-SA 3.0 license.
It is somewhat larger than the original (increase in radius of 2m) and features more player spawns and a more detailed floor along with light flag and radius changes for the Tesseract engine.
